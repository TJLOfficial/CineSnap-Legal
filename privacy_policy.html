Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CineSnap ("we", "us", or "our") handles your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

CineSnap operates entirely locally on your device. When you select a video to extract frames:

  • The video file is processed directly on your phone's processor.
  • The extracted images are saved directly to your phone's internal storage (Gallery).
  • No video or image data is ever sent to any external server or cloud service.

2. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Read Media/Storage: To allow you to select videos from your gallery.
  • Write Storage: To save the extracted images to your device.

These permissions are used strictly for the app's core functionality.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The free version of CineSnap displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data for ad performance

For more information on how Google handles your data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

Premium users do not see any ads, and no ad-related data is collected for them.
